Methods

Notetaking with wikilinks and LaTeX in markdown allows for a variety of research workflows. The approach taken in this demonstration will involve funneling information into scratch notes for each class. One can type or paste from a snip of the final Equation sheet of Alan Guth's 2012 ocw.E&M. II:

In Markdown with LaTeX block:

## Index Notation
$$
\begin{array}{r}
\vec{A} \cdot \vec{B}=A_{i} B_{i}, \quad \vec{A} \times \vec{B}_{i}=\epsilon_{i j k} A_{j} B_{k}, \quad \epsilon_{i j k} \epsilon_{p q k}=\delta_{i p} \delta_{j q}-\delta_{i q} \delta_{j p} \\
\operatorname{det} A=\epsilon_{i_{1} i_{2} \cdots i_{n}} A_{1, i_{1}} A_{2, i_{2}} \cdots A_{n, i_{n}}
\end{array}
$$
